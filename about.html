5+ Years Teaching

From Medical Studies in Ukraine to Teaching German in Berlin

My journey began in India, continued through medical studies in Ukraine, and brought me to Berlin, Germany — where I discovered that language is the true gateway to every opportunity.

After completing 5 years of medical studies and earning my B2 German certification, I recognized that thousands of Indian students and professionals struggled with the same barrier I once faced: the German language.

That's why I founded Gateway to Future — to provide CEFR-aligned, culturally relevant German language education specifically designed for Indian learners aiming for Ausbildung, Studienkolleg, or career success in Germany.

Today I teach A1 through C1 levels at LUX Lernförderung and through my own platform, combining real classroom experience with digital content that reaches students globally.
