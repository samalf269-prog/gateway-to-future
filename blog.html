Common Questions

FAQ

Is the trial lesson really free? +

Yes — completely free, no credit card required. It's a 30-minute session to assess your level and plan your learning path.

How are lessons conducted? +

All lessons are online via Zoom or Google Meet. You can attend from anywhere in India or Germany.

What materials do I need? +

Nothing — all course materials (PDFs, workbooks, flashcards) are provided by Gateway to Future at no extra cost for enrolled students.

How long to reach B1 from zero? +

With 5–6 hours per week of study, most students reach B1 within 9–12 months. Consistent practice is the key factor.

Do you offer group classes? +

Yes — weekly group sessions (max 8 students) are available at A1, A2, and B1 levels at a reduced price compared to 1:1 tutoring.
